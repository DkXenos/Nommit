05 · Rules of the Zine
Outline everything
3–6px ink border + a hard offset shadow (0 4–9px 0 #1E1712), no blur. Every card, chip, sticker and button gets the treatment so it reads as a cut-out.
Tilt ±2–4°
Stickers and cards sit at slight rotations for the scrapbook feel. Keep the reading order dead straight — chaos in decoration, order in layout.
Texture & tearing
Halftone dot grain over color fields, torn-paper diagonal hatching on photo placeholders. Warm cream + ink stay the constant frame — max 1–2 background colors.
Gummy motion
Springy squash-and-overshoot, like it's edible: the launch chomp, chip jiggle, pins bouncing in with steam, a crumb burst on Nom, cards re-dealing on sort.
